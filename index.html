About Me

Ever since I was young, I have wanted to be an inventor and make the world a better place. This pursuit led me to Georgia Tech, where I studied mechanical engineering. During my studies, I developed a passion for mechanical design, electronics, and coding. Luckily, a career exists that combines all these interests, robotics. To gain additional skills and be exposed to the cutting edge in this field, I pursued an MS in Robotics, Automation, and Controls. My primary interests in robotics are autonomous vehicles, medical robotics, and advanced manufacturing.

Outside of my professional interests, I have personal hobbies as well. One of my biggest passions is traveling. I had the great fortune of growing up with a dad who worked for a major airline. My dad's best perk is free airfare for the whole family (we fly standby, so vacations can sometimes be interesting)! I am currently in the process of completing my travel goal of seeing all 50 states before I turn 24. I also enjoy anything that has to do with the outdoors, such as tennis, hiking, sailing, and skiing. Finally, I love music. I've been playing piano for 18 years and was in a band in college.
